Amazon Prime Sorter Extension

What This Extension Does

Amazon Prime Sorter is a browser extension that enhances your Amazon shopping experience by automatically:

  • Applying the Prime delivery filter to show only Prime-eligible products
  • Sorting search results by price (low to high) to help you find the best deals
  • Working silently in the background without interrupting your browsing
🔒 Privacy-First Design: This extension operates entirely within your browser. No data is sent to our servers (because we don't have any). All settings are stored locally in your browser's storage.

Data & Privacy GDPR Compliant

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information. This includes:

  • No browsing history tracking
  • No search queries logged
  • No purchase data collected
  • No cookies set by the extension
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No fingerprinting or device identification

Local Storage Only

The extension uses your browser's built-in storage (chrome.storage.sync) to save your preferences, such as:

  • Whether the extension is enabled or disabled
  • Which marketplaces you've enabled
  • Your notification preferences

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by the extension.

DOM Interaction

The extension works by interacting with Amazon's webpage structure (DOM). It:

  • Reads the current page URL to detect search results
  • Clicks filter elements on your behalf (just like you would)
  • Modifies form submissions to include Prime parameters
  • Displays optional notification toasts
